# Northeastern Theme Park Engineering Club

Northeastern’s Theme Park Engineering Club is a dynamic community for those passionate about the intersection of art, science, and engineering in the world of theme park design.

Whether you’re fascinated by the intricate science behind attraction design, captivated by the artistry of creating immersive experiences, or simply a roller coaster enthusiast, our club offers something for everyone. We delve into the physics, engineering, and illusions that power some of the world’s most iconic rides. Members have the opportunity to engage in hands-on projects, collaborate on innovative designs, and compete in both in-house challenges and prestigious national competitions. Join us as we explore the magic of theme park engineering and push the boundaries of what’s possible!
